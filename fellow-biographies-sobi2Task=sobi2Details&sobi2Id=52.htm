Chengyao Shen

National University of Singapore
Webpage


Chengyao Shen (沈程杳) is currently a PhD candidate in the Vision and Machine Learning Lab, National University of Singapore, Singapore. He received a BS degree in microelectronics from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China in 2010.


He received the Irving T. Ho Memorial Scholarship when he was an undergraduate student at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2009 and has been a recipient of the NGS Scholarship since 2010.


He is interested in computational modeling of human visual system. His research interests include bio-inspired computer vision, machine learning, and natural image statistics.
